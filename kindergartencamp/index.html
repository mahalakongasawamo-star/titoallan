Safety & Facilities

Is My Child Safe at KCLC?

Yes. Every KCLC campus in Antipolo and Marikina is designed so children can learn, play, and grow in a secure, supervised environment. Your child's safety is non-negotiable at both locations.

Secured Campus Access

Controlled entry and exit points at both Antipolo and Marikina campuses ensure only authorized individuals can enter during school hours.

Low Student-to-Teacher Ratio

Small class sizes mean every child receives individual attention, proper supervision, and personalized instruction.

Child-Safe Classrooms

Age-appropriate furniture, rounded edges, non-toxic materials, and clean, well-ventilated learning spaces throughout.

Health & Hygiene Protocols

Regular sanitation schedules, handwashing stations, and clear health protocols keep every campus safe for every child.

Structured Daily Routine

A consistent schedule with clear transitions between activities gives children security, predictability, and emotional stability.

Trained & Certified Staff
