What Is the Early Learning Program?

KCLC's Early Learning Program gives toddlers and young children a joyful introduction to learning through multi-sensory activities, guided play, and the foundations of reading and numeracy.

Enroll Now All Programs

What Will My Toddler Learn at KCLC?

At KCLC, learning begins the moment your child walks through our doors. Our Early Learning Program is designed for children ages 2 to 4 and centers on creating a print-rich environment where the foundations of reading develop naturally. Through storytelling, rhyming activities, and exposure to letters and words in everyday contexts, your toddler will begin to understand that words carry meaning and that reading is a joyful, rewarding experience.

But reading is just the beginning. Our multi-sensory approach engages every intelligence. Children explore textures, colors, sounds, and movement through hands-on activities guided by our experienced teachers. Art projects, music, outdoor play, and nature exploration are woven into each day, ensuring that your child is stimulated across all areas of development — linguistic, spatial, bodily-kinesthetic, musical, and interpersonal.

Every activity is chosen to be developmentally appropriate, challenging enough to encourage growth but supportive enough to build confidence. Our small class sizes of 10 to 12 students ensure that every child receives the individual attention they need to thrive.

What Are the Learning Outcomes?

By the end of the Early Learning Program, your child will have developed a strong foundation across multiple areas:

  • Early Literacy — Recognize letters, understand that print carries meaning, and develop a genuine love for books and storytelling
  • Pre-Numeracy Skills — Count with understanding, recognize basic shapes, and begin exploring number relationships through hands-on manipulatives
  • Social Confidence — Interact comfortably with peers and adults, share, take turns, and express feelings appropriately
  • Fine and Gross Motor Skills — Hold a crayon correctly, cut with scissors, run, climb, and balance with increasing coordination
  • Environmental Awareness — Show curiosity about nature, develop early habits of caring for plants and animals, and understand their place in the community
  • Independence and Self-Direction — Follow simple routines, make choices, and take pride in completing tasks on their own

How Does Early Learning Prepare My Child?

The Early Learning Program is specifically designed as a bridge to the KCLC Kindergarten Program. Every skill your toddler develops here — from early reading and math fundamentals to social confidence and self-direction — lays the groundwork for the more structured academic learning that follows.

Children who complete our Early Learning Program enter kindergarten with a significant advantage: they are already comfortable in a classroom environment, they understand routines, they have a vocabulary enriched by months of storytelling and conversation, and they approach new challenges with curiosity rather than hesitation.

Our teachers carefully track each child's progress through the multiple intelligences framework, ensuring that the transition to kindergarten is smooth and that no child is left behind. When your child is ready to move up, they will do so with confidence, enthusiasm, and a genuine love for learning.
