What Is Included in KCLC Daycare?

KCLC Daycare is more than just childcare — it is an extension of our learning philosophy into the hours beyond regular class time. We provide a safe, supervised, and nurturing environment where children ages 2 to 6 can continue to grow, explore, and develop throughout the day.

Every daycare session includes a balance of structured learning activities that reinforce the skills taught in our Early Learning and Kindergarten programs. Children engage in guided reading time, creative art projects, music and movement activities, and hands-on science exploration — all under the supervision of trained, caring teachers.

Balanced, nutritious meals and snacks are provided as part of the daycare service, ensuring that children are well-nourished and energized throughout the day. We understand that nutrition plays a critical role in a child's ability to learn and stay focused, which is why our meals are planned with both health and enjoyment in mind.

Our daycare spaces are designed to be warm, inviting, and child-safe. Rest periods are built into the daily schedule for younger children, and every activity is supervised to ensure the well-being of each child in our care.

What Are the Benefits of KCLC Daycare?

Choosing KCLC Daycare means your child receives more than supervision — they receive continued development in a trusted, professional learning environment:

  • Continued Learning — Structured activities reinforce and extend what children learn in their regular programs, preventing learning loss during extended hours
  • Social Development — Children interact with peers in a guided setting, building friendships, cooperation, and communication skills throughout the day
  • Safe and Supervised — Trained teachers maintain a secure environment with proper adult-to-child ratios, giving parents complete peace of mind
  • Balanced Nutrition — Nutritious meals and snacks are provided, ensuring children have the energy they need to stay engaged and happy
  • Flexible Scheduling — Extended hours and flexible arrangements accommodate the demands of working parents, making drop-off and pick-up convenient
  • Seamless Transition — Children enrolled in our academic programs can transition directly into daycare after class, maintaining the same trusted environment and familiar teachers

What Does a Typical Day Look Like?

A day in KCLC Daycare is thoughtfully structured to balance learning, play, rest, and nourishment. Here is an overview of what your child can expect:

  • Morning Arrival and Free Play — Children settle in with age-appropriate toys, puzzles, and books while classmates arrive
  • Guided Learning Activity — Structured sessions focused on literacy, numeracy, or creative arts that complement the regular curriculum
  • Snack Time — A healthy, balanced snack to keep energy levels up
  • Outdoor or Active Play — Physical activities, nature exploration, and group games that develop gross motor skills and social interaction
  • Lunch — A nutritious, well-balanced meal served in a family-style setting
  • Rest and Quiet Time — A calm period for napping (younger children) or quiet activities like drawing and storybook browsing
  • Afternoon Activity — Creative projects, music, sensory play, or thematic activities that spark curiosity and imagination
  • Afternoon Snack and Pick-Up — A light snack followed by free play while children are picked up by parents

The schedule is flexible and adapts to the age and needs of each child. Our teachers ensure smooth transitions between activities and provide individual attention throughout the day.
