Admissions

How Do I Enroll My Child at KCLC?

Joining the KCLC family is simple. Our admissions team guides you through every step, from your first campus visit to your child's welcome orientation.

Book a Tour View Requirements

Step by Step

What Is the Enrollment Process?

1

Book a Tour

Schedule a visit to either KCLC Antipolo or KCLC Marikina. Meet our teachers, explore the campus, and see our learning spaces in action.

2

Submit Application

Complete the enrollment form online or at the campus. Provide the required documents including your child's birth certificate and recent photos.

3

Confirmation & Payment

Our admissions team reviews your application and confirms your child's slot. Pay the reservation fee via bank transfer or online payment to secure enrollment.

4

Welcome Orientation

Attend our welcome orientation to meet classmates, teachers, and staff. Learn about the daily routine, school policies, and everything your child needs to thrive.

What to Prepare

What Are the Admission Requirements?

To enroll your child at KCLC, please prepare the following documents. Our admissions team is happy to assist you throughout the process.

  • ✓ Birth Certificate — PSA-issued or certified true copy of your child's birth certificate
  • ✓ Recent ID Photos — Two (2) recent 2x2 photos of the child with a white background
  • ✓ Completed Application Form — Available for download or fill out online through our enrollment page
  • ✓ Medical Records — Health and immunization records or a medical clearance from your child's pediatrician

Have questions about requirements? Contact our admissions team and we will assist you.

Fees & Payment

What Are the Tuition and Payment Options?

Tuition fees at KCLC vary depending on the program and level your child enrolls in. Our programs include Early Learning (ages 2-4), Kindergarten (ages 4-6), and Daycare. Our admissions team will provide a detailed breakdown of fees during your campus visit or upon inquiry.

To reserve your child's slot, a reservation fee is required upon confirmation of enrollment. This fee is applied toward the total tuition balance, ensuring your child's place in the class is secured.

We accept payments through bank transfer and online payment for your convenience. Our team can walk you through the payment process and provide the necessary account details.
