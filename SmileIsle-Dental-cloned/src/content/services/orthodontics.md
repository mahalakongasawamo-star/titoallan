# Orthodontics at Smile Isle

We offer a full range of orthodontic treatments tailored to every age and need — from early intervention for young children to comprehensive care for teens and adults.

## Types of Braces

- **Traditional Metal Braces** — reliable, effective, and more comfortable than ever
- **Clear Ceramic Braces** — a more discreet option with the same great results
- **Custom Braces** — AI-powered bracket placement for precision and efficiency

## Who We Treat

- **Kids** — Early evaluations starting at age 6-7
- **Teens** — Braces or Invisalign with fun, engaging care
- **Adults** — It's never too late for the smile you deserve
