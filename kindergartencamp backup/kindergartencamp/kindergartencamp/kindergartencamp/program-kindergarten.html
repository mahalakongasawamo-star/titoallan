What Is the Kindergarten Program?

KCLC's Kindergarten Program is where reading mastery, math confidence, and big school readiness come together through an integrated, thematic curriculum built on the theory of multiple intelligences.

Enroll Now All Programs

What Does the Kindergarten Curriculum Cover?

The KCLC Kindergarten Program builds on the foundations established in our Early Learning Program and takes children to the next level of academic confidence. At its core is our renowned Beginning Reading Program, which goes far beyond simple letter recognition. Children are immersed in a print-rich environment where storytelling, intellectually stimulating materials, and daily reading practice develop not just the ability to decode words, but the ability to read with understanding and critical thinking.

Our numeracy program is aligned with world-renowned math curricula from Singapore and the United Kingdom. Children use a variety of hands-on manipulatives to move beyond rote memorization and into a deep understanding of number relationships, problem-solving strategies, and calculation proficiency. They learn to speak the language of mathematics and confidently explain their ideas to peers and adults.

All subjects are taught through integrated thematic teaching, which means that reading, math, science, social studies, and the arts are connected through meaningful themes drawn from the reality of children's daily lives. This approach, combined with the infusion of Dr. Howard Gardner's multiple intelligences theory, ensures that every child's unique learning style is honored and developed.

Caring for the Environment and Social Studies remain at the core of the kindergarten experience. Children explore their community, learn about the natural world, and develop a genuine concern for people and the environment around them.

What Skills Will My Child Gain?

By the end of the Kindergarten Program, your child will have developed a comprehensive set of skills that prepare them for success in grade school and beyond:

  • Reading Mastery — Read fluently with comprehension, retell stories, and think critically about what they read
  • Math Proficiency — Solve problems using Singapore/UK-aligned strategies, understand number relationships, and perform calculations confidently
  • Writing and Communication — Express ideas in writing, form sentences, and communicate clearly with peers and adults
  • Scientific Inquiry — Ask questions, make observations, form simple hypotheses, and understand cause-and-effect relationships
  • Social-Emotional Maturity — Manage emotions, resolve conflicts peacefully, collaborate with classmates, and show empathy for others
  • Independence and Persistence — Follow multi-step instructions, stay on task, complete assignments, and face challenges with confidence
  • Environmental Stewardship — Demonstrate awareness of and responsibility for their environment, community, and the natural world
  • Creative Expression — Use art, music, movement, and dramatic play to express ideas and explore different perspectives

Is My Child Ready for Grade School After KCLC?

Absolutely. KCLC graduates are primed for learning and success in grade school. Our curriculum provides the perfect blend of fun, engaging activities and strong academic foundations in Reading and Math that give children the edge they need when they enter their chosen big schools.

But big school readiness at KCLC goes far beyond academics. We focus equally on developing enhanced socio-emotional skills and independence. Children learn to believe in themselves, manage their time, follow routines, and persist in tasks independently. They develop all aspects of their physical, socio-emotional, and cognitive skills, building enough confidence to face any challenge.

The majority of KCLC graduates confidently cope with the demands of their chosen big schools because they are strong readers with a solid foundation in mathematics and a well-rounded set of life skills. Our track record over 14 years speaks for itself — KCLC children are ready, confident, and eager to continue their learning journey.

Frequently Asked Questions About Kindergarten

The Early Learning Program (ages 2-4) focuses on foundational skills — introducing letters, numbers, social interaction, and sensory exploration. The Kindergarten Program (ages 4-6) builds on these foundations with more structured academic learning, including reading mastery, Singapore/UK-aligned math, writing, and comprehensive big school readiness preparation. Both programs use the same progressive philosophy and multiple intelligences approach.

While completing our Early Learning Program provides a strong advantage, it is not a strict requirement. Children entering the Kindergarten Program will be assessed to determine their readiness and placed at the appropriate level. Our teachers are experienced at helping children who are new to KCLC adjust and catch up quickly. Contact us to discuss your child's specific situation.

KCLC's math program is aligned with the Singapore and UK math curricula, which are recognized worldwide for producing students who deeply understand mathematical concepts rather than just memorizing procedures. Children use hands-on manipulatives to explore number relationships, develop problem-solving strategies, and build calculation proficiency. This approach gives KCLC graduates a significant advantage when they encounter math in grade school.
