About KCLC

What Should You Know About KCLC?

Kindergarten Camp Learning Center (KCLC) is a progressive preschool with campuses in Antipolo City and Marikina City, Philippines. With over 14 years of experience, KCLC provides early childhood education grounded in Dr. Howard Gardner's theory of multiple intelligences, nurturing confident and independent learners prepared for big school.

KCLC has been providing progressive preschool education for over 14 years. Under the leadership of School Directress Ms. Lala Ticzon-Santos, the school has built a strong reputation for producing confident readers and big-school-ready graduates across its two campuses.

KCLC has two campuses conveniently located in Metro Manila's eastern corridor:

  • KCLC Antipolo — 47 Ticzon Park, P. Oliveros Street, Antipolo City, 1870 Rizal
  • KCLC Marikina — 103 Starlite St., Rancho 3 Estate, Concepcion Dos, Marikina City

Visit our Contact page for directions and to schedule a campus tour.

KCLC follows a progressive approach focusing on content-based instruction and interactive, integrative methods. The curriculum adopts integrated thematic teaching and infuses Dr. Howard Gardner's theory of multiple intelligences to discover each child's unique learning style. Caring for the Environment and Social Studies are core pillars of everything we teach.

Programs & Learning

What Programs Does KCLC Offer?

Children can start at KCLC as early as 2 years old through the Early Learning program. Programs are designed to be developmentally appropriate for each age group, from toddlers through kindergarten-age children up to 6 years old.

KCLC offers three main programs to serve families at every stage of early childhood:

  • Early Learning — For children ages 2 to 4, building foundational skills through play and exploration
  • Kindergarten — For children ages 4 to 6, with strong academic foundations in reading and math
  • Daycare — A nurturing environment for young children during working hours

KCLC is well-known for its Beginning Reading Program. Children are exposed to a print-rich environment, storytelling, and intellectually stimulating materials from the toddler level. Students develop critical thinking skills and learn to read with understanding, which is why the majority of KCLC graduates confidently handle the demands of their chosen big schools.

KCLC's Numeracy program emphasizes hands-on activities using a variety of manipulatives, making math learning meaningful and fun. The approach is compatible with world-renowned math curricula from Singapore and the UK. Children learn to make patterns, identify connections, and confidently explain math concepts to peers and adults.

Enrollment & Admissions

How Does Enrollment at KCLC Work?

Enrollment at KCLC follows a simple 4-step process: book a campus tour, submit your application with required documents, confirm enrollment and pay the reservation fee via bank transfer or online payment, and attend the welcome orientation. Visit our Admissions page for the complete step-by-step guide.

Yes, KCLC offers online enrollment for the convenience of parents. You can fill out the enrollment form on our website to reserve a slot, then pay the reservation or enrollment fees through online payment or bank transfer. Our admissions team will guide you through each step of the process.

Parents need to prepare the following documents for enrollment:

  • Birth Certificate — PSA-issued or certified true copy
  • Recent ID Photos — Two (2) recent 2x2 photos with white background
  • Completed Application Form — Available online or at the campus
  • Medical Records — Health and immunization records or pediatric clearance

Visit our Admissions page for full details on each requirement.

Yes, KCLC encourages parents to book a school tour before enrolling. During the visit, you can meet our teachers, observe classroom activities, and explore the campus. Tours are available at both KCLC Antipolo and KCLC Marikina during office hours, Monday to Saturday, 7:30 AM to 5:30 PM.

School Life

What Is Life Like at KCLC?

KCLC office hours are Monday to Saturday, 7:30 AM to 5:30 PM. Class schedules may vary depending on the program and level. Contact our admissions team or visit the campus for the specific class schedule that applies to your child's age group.

Child safety is a top priority at KCLC. Our campuses are designed with age-appropriate facilities, secure entry and exit points, and a controlled environment. Our experienced teachers and staff maintain close supervision throughout the school day to ensure every child feels safe, secure, and cared for.
