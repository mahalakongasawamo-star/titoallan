About KCLC

Who Is Behind KCLC?

Kindergarten Camp Learning Center was founded in 2010 by Ms. Lala Ticzon-Santos and has shaped young minds in Antipolo and Marikina for 14+ years. Our team of passionate early childhood educators builds self-directed, socially aware, and environmentally conscious learners through progressive, child-centered education.

Our Story

How Did Kindergarten Camp Start?

Kindergarten Camp Learning Center was founded by Ms. Lala Ticzon-Santos, a dedicated educator with a clear vision: to build a school where children develop not just academically, but as whole human beings. From the very beginning, KCLC adopted a progressive approach to education, grounded in Dr. Howard Gardner's theory of multiple intelligences.

For over 14 years, KCLC has served families in Antipolo and Marikina through integrated thematic teaching, content-based instruction, and developmentally appropriate methods. The curriculum places caring for the environment and social studies at its core, ensuring children see meaningful connections between what they learn and the world around them.

Today, KCLC operates two campuses and continues its mission as "your child's learning camp of explorations" — a place where every child is empowered to become a confident, independent learner ready for whatever comes next.

Mission & Values

What Does KCLC Stand For?

Self-Directed Learning

Children are guided to become independent thinkers who are ready to learn, explore, and maximize their potential through hands-on, developmentally appropriate experiences.

Social Transformation

KCLC develops learners who show genuine concern for humanity and community. Children grow into socially aware individuals geared towards positive change in their environment.

Environmental Care

Caring for the environment sits at the core of everything KCLC teaches. Children learn to respect nature and understand their responsibility as stewards of the world they live in.

Safety & Facilities

Is KCLC a Safe Environment for Children?

Secure Campus

Both campuses are fully secured with controlled access, ensuring only authorized individuals enter the premises during school hours.

Small Class Sizes

We maintain small class sizes so every child receives personalized attention and care from their teachers throughout the day.

Trained Staff

All KCLC educators are trained in early childhood education, child safety protocols, and progressive teaching methodologies.

Clean Facilities

Classrooms, play areas, and common spaces are regularly cleaned and sanitized to maintain a healthy learning environment for every child.

Parent Communication

Parents stay informed through regular updates, direct contact with teachers, and open communication channels about their child's progress and well-being.

Emergency Protocols

KCLC has clear emergency procedures in place, with regular drills and trained personnel ready to respond to any situation swiftly and safely.
