Frequently asked questions

Stage 01 — Before You Buy
What exactly does Upserv do?

Upserv builds AI-friendly websites designed so that AI platforms like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and others can read, understand, and recommend your business to their users.

Why is my current website invisible to AI?

Most websites are built for human visitors and Google crawlers, not AI language models. AI needs structured data, schema markup, and verified fact statements to recommend businesses accurately.

Is Upserv the same as SEO?

No. SEO optimizes for Google's traditional search rankings. Upserv optimizes for AI recommendation engines — a completely different system with different requirements.

How much does an Upserv website cost?

Starting at $1,000 for a Starter package. Growth is $5,000 and Scale is $10,000. All tiers require a $150/month data subscription.

Stage 02 — Getting Started
How long does it take to get my website live?

Typical turnaround depends on the tier selected. Most Starter sites launch within 2-3 weeks. Growth and Scale projects take 4-8 weeks.

Do I need to replace my current website?

Not necessarily. Upserv can rebuild your existing site with AI-optimized structure or create a new one from scratch, depending on your needs.

What is a training document?

A training document is a plain-language fact file about your business that AI platforms can ingest and cite accurately. It contains verified data about your services, pricing, hours, and credentials.

Stage 03 — After Launch
What is a Ghost Score?

A Ghost Score measures how visible your business is across AI platforms. It shows whether AI can find, understand, and recommend your business accurately.

How do I know if AI visibility is working?

We deliver visibility reports showing your business's presence across AI platforms, and refresh your data every 30 days as part of the subscription.

What happens after my website launches?

After launch, Upserv manages your AI visibility on a $150/month subscription. Every 30 days we:

  • Refresh your training document across all 19 platforms
  • Update any changed services, pricing, or hours
  • Add new reviews and trust signals
  • Send a monthly visibility report with your Ghost Score and AI mentions
