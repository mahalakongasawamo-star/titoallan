## FAQs section (`id="faqs"`)

- **H2**: FAQ
- **Subheading / eyebrow**: Our approach

Ten `<details>/<summary>` accordions.

### FAQ 1
- **Q**: Do you work on brand strategy and large-scale projects?
- **A**: Yes. We support brands in France and internationally, on matters of positioning, brand architecture, identity and global storytelling. We are used to leading complex, large-scale transformations with both strategic clarity and meticulous operational attention.

### FAQ 2
- **Q**: Are you specialised in packaging?
- **A**: Yes. Packaging design lies at the heart of our expertise — whether for creation, redesign, innovation or strategic evolution.

### FAQ 3
- **Q**: Do you work beyond packaging?
- **A**: Yes. We activate other essential dimensions of the brand: platform, narrative, visual territories, motion, retail environments, digital ecosystems and more.

### FAQ 4
- **Q**: Do you collaborate with R&D or innovation teams?
- **A**: Yes. We intervene upstream, alongside innovation and research teams, to reveal the sensorial and differentiating potential of emerging technologies. The ambition is to express innovation differently — through brand, design, materiality or narrative.

### FAQ 5
- **Q**: Are you a large agency?
- **A**: We are a highly expert and agile structure, surrounding ourselves with bespoke talents depending on each project's scale and complexity.

### FAQ 6
- **Q**: What differentiates you from a traditional agency?
- **A**: We cultivate a relational, refined and strategic approach. No standardised methods — only bespoke, confidential and intuitive vision.

### FAQ 7
- **Q**: What is your working process?
- **A**: A fluid three-step journey: immersion, revelation, materialisation — always co-constructed with our clients.

### FAQ 8
- **Q**: Do you work with responsible brands?
- **A**: Yes — it is one of our driving forces. We support brands that carry a sincere awareness of their environmental, societal or cultural impact, and seek to express it with coherence and depth.

### FAQ 9
- **Q**: Do you work internationally?
- **A**: Always. Yes. Our strategic approach is designed to travel — while remaining sensitive to cultural nuances and local specificities.

### FAQ 10
- **Q**: How do you embody your values beyond client projects?
- **A**: Our commitment extends beyond brand strategy. Through initiatives such as The Providence Club, we create spaces for dialogue between leaders, creatives and innovators around the positive transformation of brands. We also contribute to events and reflections on the future of branding — fostering a more human, conscious and inspired ecosystem.
